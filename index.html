About

Round Penguin studio is small studio based in Auckland. We are interested in making fantasy, fairy tale type games and other media.

Games

   

Survival of the Fattest is an online multiplayer game where the player begins as a juvenile blob, eating their way into evolving to three different fluffy creatures, each with their own characteristics and skill tree. The goal of the game is to eat as much as possible and be the last to survive.
